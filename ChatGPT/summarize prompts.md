# Advanced Prompt Alternatives to “Summarize This”

Instead of saying **“summarize this,”** use the following high-value prompts to generate deeper, more strategic insights:

---

## 1. Extract Strategic Insights

> **Prompt:**  
> *“Analyse this text like a McKinsey consultant. Identify the key ideas, untapped opportunities, and high-impact areas where I should focus my efforts. Number them for clarity.”*

**Best for:** Strategic planning, leadership reviews, business positioning, growth analysis.

---

## 2. Extract What Others Overlook

> **Prompt:**  
> *“Review this content and highlight the unspoken assumptions, hidden biases, and subtle perceptions that casual readers would overlook but subject matter experts would catch.”*

**Best for:** Critical analysis, risk assessment, due diligence, academic or policy review.

---

## 3. Summarise Complex Research

> **Prompt:**  
> *“Break down the research methodology, main conclusions, and study limitations of this scientific paper point by point. Then craft a concise three-sentence overview. Emphasise how the findings can be translated into real-world applications.”*

**Best for:** Academic papers, whitepapers, technical research, industry reports.

---

## 4. Executive Actionable Summary

> **Prompt:**  
> *“As a seasoned project leader, distill the essential points from the report in roughly 200 words, complete with at least three concrete next steps.”*

**Best for:** Leadership briefings, board updates, operational reviews.

---

## 5. Policy Brief – Key Points

> **Prompt:**  
> *“Provide a bulleted summary of the attached policy documents covering the primary goals, recommended approaches, and anticipated obstacles in about 100 words.”*

**Best for:** Government documents, regulatory updates, compliance reviews.
