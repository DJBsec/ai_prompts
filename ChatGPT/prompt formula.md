# AI Prompt Formula

A simple structure for writing powerful, high-clarity prompts:


[Role] + [Context] + [Task] + [Output] + [Reference]
Use this formula to consistently generate precise, relevant, and high-quality responses.

---

## 1. Role

**What it means:**  
Define who the AI should act as so it responds with the right expertise and perspective.

**Why it matters:**  
Framing the role shapes tone, depth, and decision-making style.

**Example:**  
> You are an experienced [market] consultant with [X] years of enterprise experience.

---

## 2. Context

**What it means:**  
Provide background information, audience details, and constraints so the response fits your situation.

**Include details like:**
- Who the audience is  
- What’s currently happening  
- Constraints (time, budget, tools, resources)  
- Goals or priorities  

**Example:**  
> This is for a board presentation. We are a [company size] [company type] company [doing x]]. Budget is limited and rollout must happen within 6 months.

---

## 3. Task

**What it means:**  
Clearly state the specific deliverable or outcome you need.

**Be explicit and measurable.**

**Example:**  
> Create a phased roadmap for the next 12 months.

---

## 4. Output

**What it means:**  
Specify the format so the response is ready to use without major edits.

**Examples of output formats:**
- Bullet points  
- Table  
- Executive summary  
- Step-by-step plan  
- Slide outline  
- Markdown document  

**Example:**  
> Provide the response as a 5-slide outline with bullet points under each slide.

---

## 5. Reference (Optional)

**What it means:**  
Provide examples, links, tone guidance, or past documents to match for consistency and accuracy.

**You can reference:**
- A previous report  
- A writing style  
- A framework  
- A specific tone (formal, persuasive, technical)  

**Example:**  
> Match the tone of a McKinsey strategy brief. Keep it concise and data-driven.

---

# Full Example Prompt

You are a senior [field] strategy consultant with [X] years of experience.

This is for a board-level presentation at a company undergoing digital transformation. Budget is constrained and leadership is risk-averse.

Create a 12-month modernization roadmap.

Provide the output as:

A high-level executive summary (150 words)

A phased roadmap table (Quarter, Initiative, Impact, Risk Level)

Three key risks with mitigation strategies

Match the tone of a top-tier consulting firm.
