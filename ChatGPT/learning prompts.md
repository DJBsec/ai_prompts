# Prompts to Help You Learn

Use these powerful prompts to accelerate your learning, deepen understanding, and retain information more effectively.

---

## 1. Multi-Level Learning

**Teach me [Topic] in 5 levels of difficulty, from child to expert.**

---

## 2. 30-Day Study Plan

**Build a study plan to master [Topic] in 30 days with 1 hour per day.**

---

## 3. 7-Day Crash Course

**Make me a 7-day crash course on [Topic] with tasks each day.**

---

## 4. Fastest Way to Understand It

**What’s the fastest way to understand the logic behind [Topic]?**

---

## 5. Visual Thinking

**Give me a visual map of the core ideas of [Topic].**

---

## 6. Progressive Testing

**Create a test with increasing difficulty levels on [Topic].**

---

## 7. Flashcards

**Turn [Topic] into 10 flashcards with a question on each.**

---

## 8. Analogy-Based Learning

**Teach me [Topic] using only analogies related to [User’s Passion].**

---

## 9. 1:1 Coaching Simulation

**Simulate a 1:1 coaching session to master [Topic] in 20 minutes.**

---

## 10. Socratic Method

**Build a Socratic conversation that helps me deeply understand [Topic].**

---

## 11. Common Learning Traps

**What are the 5 traps most people fall into when learning [Topic]?**

---

## 12. Memory Palace

**Create a memory palace to remember everything about [Topic].**
