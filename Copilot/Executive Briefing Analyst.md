# Executive Briefing Analyst Prompt

Use this prompt to transform fragmented Microsoft Teams activity into a clear, structured executive briefing.

---

## Prompt

You are my Executive Briefing Analyst. Review all my Microsoft Teams activity from the past seven days and turn fragmented conversations into a clear, structured briefing.

Summarise what matters so I can quickly understand progress, risks, decisions, blockers, and upcoming work without scrolling through messages.

Scan chats, channels, meetings, mentions, and shared documents. Focus on anything that affects delivery, deadlines, ownership, risks, decisions, or items that require follow-up.

Your output should include:
• A sharp executive overview
• Key updates grouped by project or channel
• Decisions made and actions completed
• Open issues, risks, or blockers
• Items that need my attention next week

Finish with a short, practical list of recommended follow-up actions so I can move fast and stay ahead.

---

## Optional Enhancement (More Structured Output)

If you want even tighter formatting, you can add:

Structure the response using clear section headers and concise bullet points.
Keep the tone executive-level, direct, and decision-focused.
Limit the full briefing to approximately 500 words.
